Minecraft

  • Developer:Mojang Studios
  • Publisher:Xbox Game Studios
  • Release Date:November 18,2011
  • Platforms:PC,PS 4,Xbox Series,Nintendo Switch
Minecraft is a sandbox game with an open world and elements of survival.Its main feature is the ability
to create everything that is suitable.Everything is limited only by your imagination.The game features a
huge number of blocks with which you can bring your idea to life.

Terraria

  • Developer: Re-Logic
  • Publisher: Re-Logic
  • Release Date: 2011
  • Platforms: PC,PS family,Xbox Series,Nintendo Switch, Android
Terraria is a open world sandbox game with a crafting and quest system.While Terraria doesn't have
a proper plot there is a goal that you as a player need to achieve.There are tons of different mobs and
bosses (even unnecessary ones) with a unique loot so there is always something new for you.


  • Interesting crafting and looting system
  • Creativity friendly building system
  • Lots of bosses
  • Modding friendly
  • While having tips and some in-game tools it still remains unfriendly for new player
  • Not exactly exciting graphic and physic

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

  • Developer: Iron Galaxy Studios
  • Publisher: Bethesda Game Studios
  • Release Date: 2011
  • Platforms: PC,PS family,Xbox Series,Nintendo Switch
Skyrim is an open world game with a mix of RPG, sandbox mechanics.Its famous for it's open world
and countless stories filling it.It's open for you to decide your own story.Chose your side or don't, join
a fraction or just rom the world, it's all up to you!


  • Huge and interesting open world
  • High replayability
  • Freedom for players with a lot of choices
  • Can be easily modded
  • Quite a few bugs
  • Some road being unbalanced
  • No multiplayer

Dota 2

  • Developer: Valve
  • Publisher: Valve
  • Release Date: 2013
  • Platforms: PC
Dota 2 is one of the first, most famous and popular MOBAs.It's well-known for wide variety of playable
characters, team-building and all strategies that player come up with.The game is always updating with
a various new mechanics and characters coming out every year and that is without mentioning the
yearly tournaments that are also biggest in the indastry.


  • Almost every game is unique without many exactly same character match-ups
  • Playing as a same character over and over again you are still unlikely to run the exact same set often
  • Community workshop with lots of new mini-games that are totally different from regular gaming style
  • OFFICIALLY THE MOST TOXIC COMMUNITY
  • Some characters being straight broken for the time between new patch
  • Bugs that aren't exactly rare
